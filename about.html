About Blacklist


Hey! Welcome to Blacklist!

Blacklist is a cutting-edge cybersecurity platform dedicated to protecting individuals, businesses, and organizations from digital threats. It provides real-time updates on emerging cyberattacks, offers educational content to boost online safety awareness, and delivers expert tools to help users identify and defend against malicious activity. Whether you're a casual user or an IT professional, Blacklist is your first line of defense in the digital world.


Get to Know the Founder!


Alyanna Fiona C. Arcilla
Founder

Blacklist was founded by Alyanna, also known as Yang, a student at FEU Institute of Technology who dared to blend the worlds of cyber defense and unapologetic self-expression. While studying cybersecurity and exploring ethical hacking, she noticed how the industry often felt cold, overly technical, and visually dull. Refusing to conform, Yang created Blacklist as a platform that defies the norm—merging high-level threat intelligence with bold aesthetics and fierce attitude. Her goal was to prove that you can be powerful, smart, and stylish all at once. Blacklist reflects her belief that digital protection doesn’t have to be boring—it can be brilliant, bold, and beautifully disruptive.
